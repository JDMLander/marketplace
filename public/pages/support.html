Support Details

If you need assistance, please contact our support team. We are here to help with any questions or issues you may have regarding your order, account, or general inquiries. Below are the answers to some common questions:


How do I place an order?

To place an order, you can either search a textbook's name, author, or ISBN code. We also have a filter system that allows you to find a textbook by its subject, price range, and current condition. After you find your desired textbook, add it to your cart. Once you are ready, proceed to checkout, fill in your shipping information, and choose your payment method to complete your order.


What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and other online payment systems.


How do I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can use this to track the status of your shipment directly from the courier's website.


How do I list a textbook?

First, click on our sell tab. Then, insert the title of the textbook, author, edition, price, subject, and condition. Next provide an image of the textbook. Once you submit this information, your textbook will be up for review.


How long will my textbook be under review?

Textbooks are typically under review for 3-5 business days. Once the status of your review is complete, you will recieve a notification.
